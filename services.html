RankOps Mobile FAQ

A few quick answers to how the app works and who it’s for.

Is the app free?

Yes — RankOps Mobile is included with active RankOps monthly packages. There’s no separate subscription.

Do I need technical skills?

No. The app is built for owners and operators. You’ll see clear metrics, lead counts, and a simple chat thread — not raw data dumps.

Can I use it without services?

Not yet. The app is tightly coupled to RankOps SEO, GEO, AI agents, and automations. If you want access, start with a package on the services page.
